Stray Dog Rescue and Care

About Us

Welcome to Love the Paw, where compassion drives our mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray dogs. Founded on kindness, our initiative has evolved from a single act of care into a community-driven effort that saves countless lives. We believe every dog deserves a loving home, providing medical care, nourishment, and safe shelter while finding forever homes for our furry friends. Beyond rescue, we raise awareness about the challenges faced by stray dogs and educate the public on responsible pet ownership. Through our dedicated team, we strive to inspire others to act with compassion and make a lasting impact.

WHAT WE OFFER

SHELTER

At our shelter, we care for homeless dogs, providing medical care, food, and temporary housing while seeking permanent homes. We also promote responsible pet ownership through community programs.

MEDICARE

We provide medical care for stray dogs, including vaccinations, treatments, and emergency services. Our goal is to ensure their health and well-being while finding them permanent homes.

AWARENESS AND EDUCATION

We raise awareness and educate communities about stray dogs, promoting responsible pet ownership, humane treatment, and the importance of spaying/neutering to reduce stray populations.
